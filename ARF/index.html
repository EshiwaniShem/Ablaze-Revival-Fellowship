Welcome

A Fellowship Born
in the Fire

Ablaze Revival Fellowship (ARF) is a vibrant, Christ-centered ministry passionate about ushering in genuine revival. We exist to see lives transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit through uncompromising prayer, worship, and the Word of God.

From our roots in Kenya, we have grown into a ministry with a presence in Nairobi, Migori, Kisii, and Busia — and our fire is still spreading.

Our Story

About Ablaze Revival
Fellowship

Born in prayer, sustained by the Spirit — we exist to see genuine revival sweep across Kenya and the nations.

Foundation

Our Vision, Mission
& Values

👁️

Our Vision

To see a generation fully on fire for God — transformed communities, revived churches, and nations impacted by the uncompromising gospel of Jesus Christ.

🎯

Our Mission

To ignite revival through prayer, worship, discipleship, and missions — equipping every believer to fulfil their God-given destiny and calling.

📖

Our Philosophy

We believe in the full gospel — the Word and the Spirit working together. Every program, every gathering, every message is designed to produce lasting spiritual transformation.

What We Stand For

Core Values

🔥

Revival-First Culture

Everything we do is fuelled by a passion for genuine Holy Spirit revival. We refuse to settle for religion when we can have relationship and power.

🙏

Prayer as Foundation

Prayer is not an add-on — it is our culture. We are a house of prayer where intercession and worship are the bedrock of all ministry activity.

📖

Biblical Integrity

We anchor every teaching, experience, and practice firmly in the Word of God. Scripture is our final authority and our compass.

🤝

Discipleship & Mentorship

We are committed to not just making converts, but disciples — individuals who are spiritually mature, missionally engaged, and kingdom-minded.

🌍

Missional Heartbeat

The Great Commission drives us. We are committed to taking the gospel beyond church walls to every community, county, and nation.

👥

Community & Belonging

We build genuine community where every person is known, valued, and cared for — because the kingdom is built on authentic relationships.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

01

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal, co-eternal, and co-substantial.

02

The Word of God

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God — the supreme standard for all faith and practice.

03

Salvation by Grace

Salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone — His death, burial, and resurrection — and is a gift of God's grace, not earned by works.

04

Baptism of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as a subsequent work of grace evidenced by the gifts of the Spirit and power for ministry.

05

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing as provided through the atonement of Christ and available to all who believe.

06

The Second Coming

We believe in the literal, personal, and imminent return of Jesus Christ for His church and His final reign as King of kings.

What We Do

Our Programs

Intentional gatherings and schools designed to produce spiritually mature, missionally engaged kingdom citizens.

🔥

Upper Room Experience

Every Saturday · 10:00 PM – 5:00 AM

Intense prayer, worship, and prophetic encounters modelled after the Acts 2 upper room. This all-night gathering is the heartbeat of ARF — where breakthroughs happen, chains break, and glory falls.


🙏

Prayer & Fasting

First Week Every Month · Monday–Friday

Corporate prayer and fasting that shifts atmospheres and moves mountains. Participants connect online and in-person for daily prayer sessions, guided fasting, and evening prayer gatherings.


⚡

School of the Supernatural

Every Tuesday · 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM

A training ground for believers to understand and operate in the gifts of the Holy Spirit, healing, prophecy, signs, and wonders. Practical, biblical, and transformative.


📖

Life Academy

Every Thursday · 6:30 PM – 9:00 PM

ARF's flagship discipleship and leadership school. A structured curriculum covering theology, ministry skills, character development, and kingdom leadership — raising the next generation of revival leaders.


📚

Bible Study

Every Wednesday · 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Deep, expository study of God's Word that builds faith, renews the mind, and equips believers to live by divine truth. Available in-person and online via Google Meet.


🌟

Youth Programs

Every Friday · 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Dynamic programs for young people aged 13–35, combining powerful worship, relevant teaching, mentorship, and intentional community. Where the next generation encounters God.


🕊️

Discipleship Classes

Every Sunday · 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM

Foundational classes for new believers covering salvation, baptism, prayer, the Word, identity in Christ, and community. Your first step to a grounded faith.


🎵

Revival Meetings

Monthly · Various Locations

Open-air and indoor revival meetings marked by powerful preaching, signs and wonders, salvations, and healing. Taking the fire of God to cities, villages, and institutions.
